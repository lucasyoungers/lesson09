Shuckle in Competetive

Possibly the most versitile Pokémon ever to live (other than smeargle), Shuckle has access to a variety of setup moves and hazards, and has the highest defenses in the game. Not only that, but it has a certain move that allows it to have insane attack stats instead.

It's common moves include Sticky Web, Stealth Rock, Toxic, Encore, Rock Tomb, Infestation, and Knock Off.
